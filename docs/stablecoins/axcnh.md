# AxCNH

## Overview

AxCNH is issued by AnchorX.KZ: regulated by the Astana Financial Services Authority (AFSA) in Kazakhstan and is available exclusively to users classified as Professional Clients under AFSA regulations. AxCNH stablecoin reserve composition and monthly attestations are publicly disclosed, assuring its 1:1 exchangeability with offshore Chinese Yuan (CNH).
