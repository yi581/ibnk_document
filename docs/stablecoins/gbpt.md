# GBPT

## Overview

**`Official Site:`** https://poundtoken.io/

GBPT is the first regulated GBP stablecoin issuer in the British Isles.

[Poundtoken](https://poundtoken.io/) is the sole issuer of GBPT and is audited monthly by [KPMG](https://kpmg.com/xx/en/home.html).

Monthly assurance reports give holders of GBPT the peace of mind that their GBPT maintains its stable value.

Designed for stability, GBPT is 100% backed by GBP held in GBP-denominated bank accounts so that it's always redeemable 1:1 for pounds sterling.
