# CADA

## Overview

**`Official Site:`** http://www.getcadc.com/

[CADC](https://paytrie.com/cadc) is issued by [PAYTRIE AB Inc.](https://paytrie.com/), a FINTRAC regulated Money Services Business ([M19690633](https://www10.fintrac-canafe.gc.ca/msb-esm/public/detailed-information/msb-details/7b226d73624f72674e756d626572223a3135393938382c227072696d617279536561726368223a7b226f72674e616d65223a2270617974726965222c2273656172636854797065223a317d7d/)), and backed by fully reserved assets, and redeemable on a 1:1 basis for Canadian dollars. PayTrie is backed by [ConsenSys Labs](https://mesh.xyz/portfolio/) and is the leader in stablecoins in Canada. PayTrie enables fiat to stablecoin conversion using Interac e-Transfers and fast, non-intrusive KYC options.
