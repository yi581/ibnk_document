# XSGD

## Overview

**`Official Site:`** https://www.straitsx.com/

XSGD The Digital Singapore Dollar enabling cheap, transparent, decentralised, and frictionless settlement in Digital Assets markets. XSGD can settle transactions instantly 24/7 in a trusted and secure manner without the need for a 3rd party clearing agent. XSGD Tokens are always convertible at a 1:1 rate against their respective ASEAN currencies on [StraitsX](https://www.straitsx.com/).

## How to Mint/Redeem

**`To mint/redeem:`** Visit [StraitsX](https://www.straitsx.com/) or [XanPool](https://xanpool.com/)
